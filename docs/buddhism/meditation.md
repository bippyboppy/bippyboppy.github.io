# Meditation
This document will cover forms of *buddhist* meditation, it will largely ignore secular, modified, watered down forms, I am religious. 



# Nembutsu[^1]
> "Namo Amida Butsu"  

The oral recitation of the name of amitabha is a pure land practice, it purifies the mind and is easier to focus on compared to visualisation practice, it's widely accepted it should be done out loud hence the oral recitation.
