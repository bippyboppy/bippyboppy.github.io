## Treadmill accuracy
This is a self test of the accuracy of an iphone tracking my runs on a treadmill, my base assumptions are 

1. The treadmill is accurate (especially compared to the phone)
2. Accuracy of iphone run tracking (on a treadmill) will increase with duration
